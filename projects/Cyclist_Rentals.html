Cyclist Rental Analytics (Google Sheets + BigQuery)

Goal: Analyze bike rentals to find demand patterns, top stations, and opportunities to improve operations.

Tools

  • BigQuery SQL
  • Google Sheets (charts)

Key Insights

  • Peak demand occurs during commute hours.
  • Top stations account for a large share of rides.
  • Casual riders show longer trip duration than members.
