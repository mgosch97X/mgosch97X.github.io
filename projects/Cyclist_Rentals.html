Cyclist Rental Analytics (Google Sheets + BigQuery)

Goal: Analyze bike rentals to find demand patterns, top stations, and opportunities to improve operations.

Tools

  • BigQuery SQL
  • Google Sheets (charts)

Key Insights

  • Peak demand occurs during commute hours.
  • Top stations account for a large share of rides.
  • Ride demand peaks mid-week, with Wednesday through Friday showing the highest number of trips.
  • Members generate most rides in both 2019 and 2020, indicating that repeat usage is driven mainly by subscription customers.
  • Casual riders show longer trip duration than members.
  • Ride demand grew year over year, suggesting increasing adoption of bike rentals in 2020.
