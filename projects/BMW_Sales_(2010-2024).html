BMW Sales 2010-2024 Analytics (Google Sheets + Tableau)

Goal: Identify regional model demand patterns and fuel trends over time.

Tools

  • Tableau Dashboard
  • Google Sheets (charts)

Key Insights

  • Model Performance varies strongly by region, with no single model dominating all markets.
  • SUV and premium models dominate overall sales volume across the 2010-2024 period.
  • Different models lead in different regions, reinforcing the importance of localized portfolios.
  • Sales volumes across fuel types fluctuate year to year, but remain relatively balanced over time.
  • The fuel mix remains stable from 2010-2024, indicating gradual rather than disruptive transitions.
