Tools

Key Insights

  • Model performance differs by region—no single model dominates all markets.
  • SUV and premium models lead total sales volume across 2010–2024.
  • Regional leaders vary, reinforcing the need for localized portfolios.
  • Fuel volumes fluctuate year-to-year, but remain relatively balanced overall.
  • Fuel mix stays stable, suggesting gradual (not disruptive) transition.
